Live Recording

YOUR PHONE IS YOUR PIT CREW

Hit Start Session and drive. PitWall uses your phone's GPS to automatically detect every lap crossing the start/finish line — no buttons, no hardware.

  • 📍
    Auto-lap detection
    GPS corridor detection triggers a new lap the moment you cross the line. A manual tap is always available as a backup.
  • ⚡
    Real-time metrics
    Top speed, average speed, G-forces, and lap delta — all computed live from your GPS trace.
  • 👻
    Ghost lap overlay
    See your previous lap traced on the live map so you can feel exactly where you're gaining or losing time.

Leaderboards

KNOW EXACTLY WHERE YOU STAND

Every track has a live leaderboard. Filter by car class so you're always comparing against the right competition. Your row is always visible, no matter where you rank.

  • 🏆
    Track-specific rankings
    Every circuit has its own all-time board. Your fastest valid lap is your entry — improve it any time you're on track.
  • 🎖
    Racer license tiers
    Rookie → Amateur → Pro-Am → Pro. Your tier advances automatically as you log more sessions, displayed as a badge on every leaderboard.
  • 🔍
    Filter by car class
    Street, Track Day, and Race categories keep comparisons fair. Your Civic belongs on a different board to a GT3 car.
